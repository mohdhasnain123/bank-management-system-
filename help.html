FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Who can avail Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme?

  1. A family comprising of husband, wife and unmarried children
  2. Beneficiary should not own a pucca house either in his / her name or in the name of any member of his / her family in any part of India.
  3. Meeting income criteria defined as per various categories under the scheme.
